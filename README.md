## Guide Types Explained

### Player Strategy Guide
Complete competitive strategy for playing *as* the faction. Covers:
- Faction identity and win conditions
- Opening, midgame, and late-game strategy
- Fleet doctrine and technology priorities
- Diplomacy and table management
- Matchup awareness
- Common mistakes to avoid

### Defensive Playbook
Tactical guidance for defending your position while playing the faction. Focuses on border security, threat assessment, and when to fight versus when to fortify.

### Anti-Faction Playbook
Counter-strategies for playing *against* the faction. Identifies vulnerabilities, optimal timing windows for pressure, and how to prevent the faction from reaching its win condition.
